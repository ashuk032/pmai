About

This demo showcases an AI-driven predictive maintenance system tailored for airport infrastructure (runway lighting, baggage units, escalators, HVAC, power subsystems). It predicts failure risk, estimates time-to-failure, and prioritizes maintenance to reduce disruptions.

StratoHack Judging Fit

  • Relevance: Targets aviation ground systems reliability and safety.
  • Innovation: What-if simulation, risk threshold tuning, minimal UX.
  • AI/ML: Classification (RF/XGB), engineered features, risk calibration-ready.
  • Execution: End-to-end: data → model → risk → UI.
  • Presentation: Clear dashboard and component drill-down.

How it works

  1. Synthetic data simulates sensor and maintenance timelines.
  2. Model predicts next-interval failure probability.
  3. Risk converts to priority and TTF estimate.
  4. UI shows alerts, filters, and what-if adjustments.
